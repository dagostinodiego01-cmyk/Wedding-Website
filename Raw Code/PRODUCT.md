# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Invited wedding guests for Diego and Bethany. They are visiting to understand the couple's story, confirm the wedding date and venue, plan travel and accommodation, and eventually submit an RSVP.

## Product Purpose

This site is a single-page static wedding website for Diego and Bethany's 29 April 2027 celebration at Southdowns Manor. It exists to create a warm first impression, share the story and schedule, reduce guest uncertainty around logistics, and prepare a simple RSVP path once hosting and response collection are connected.

## Brand Personality

Romantic, botanical, fresh, personal, and countryside-refined, carried by a bright spring palette. The voice should feel intimate and celebratory without becoming overly formal, generic, or decorative for its own sake.

## Anti-references

Avoid gray, flat, static interfaces. Avoid generic wedding templates, lifeless placeholder-heavy layouts, and styling that feels disconnected from the couple's story, the countryside venue, or the fresh spring sky-blue and garden-green palette present in the site.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the couple, not the template.
- Make logistics calm and easy to scan.
- Let botanical detail support the story instead of becoming decoration alone.
- Preserve warmth while keeping text readable and interactions clear.
- Add movement only where it makes the page feel alive and celebratory.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use solid accessibility defaults: responsive layout, keyboard-visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and readable contrast for body text, form labels, placeholders, and calls to action. No additional guest-specific accessibility requirements are known yet.
